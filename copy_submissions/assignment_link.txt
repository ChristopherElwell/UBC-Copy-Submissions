1.  Navigate to the course you would like to take the assignment from. Then, click on the ‘Assignments’ tab on the left side of the page. 

2.  Click into the assignment you would like to copy submissions on. 

3.  Then, copy the URL. The format should include: https://canvas.ubc.ca/courses/11111/assignments/1111111, with the 1’s being replaced by your course and assignment’s unique codes. 

4.  Paste this link into the tool.
